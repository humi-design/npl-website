About PrecisionBolt

FORGING THE FUTURE

Nirmal Precision Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Nirmal Industries), established in 1997 in Bhayandar, has been a trusted name in the fastener manufacturing industry in India. Since 2006, the company has been operating under its current name and is strategically located in Thane district near Mumbai suburbs, ensuring excellent connectivity and logistics support.

We specialize in the manufacturing of high-quality stainless steel and steel fasteners, engineered with precision to meet global standards. Our product range includes machined fasteners from M4 to M36, manufactured in compliance with international DIN standards, ensuring durability, accuracy, and superior performance.

With a strong commitment to quality, precision engineering, and customer satisfaction, Nirmal Precision Pvt. Ltd. serves a wide range of industries, delivering reliable fastening solutions tailored to diverse applications.

We are a leading manufacturer of steel and stainless steel fasteners, known for our commitment to quality, precision, and innovation. As part of our growth strategy, we are actively expanding our product range and increasing our footprint across Europe and the USA, supplying DIN-standard fasteners that meet global industry demands.

Your Trusted Partner for High-Performance Fastener Solutions

Nirmal Precision Pvt. Ltd. is a leading fastener manufacturer in India, specializing in steel and stainless steel fasteners designed for high-performance industrial applications. Our focused expertise enables us to deliver precision-engineered fastening solutions that meet the most demanding global quality standards.

Specialization in Steel & Stainless Steel Fasteners

Our core strength lies in manufacturing high-tensile steel fasteners and corrosion-resistant stainless steel fasteners, ensuring superior durability, load-bearing capacity, and long-term reliability. By concentrating exclusively on these materials, we achieve: Enhanced material strength and fatigue resistance Superior corrosion resistance (SS 304, SS 316 grades) Consistent thread accuracy and dimensional precision Compliance with DIN, ISO, ASTM, and international standards Our product range includes bolts, nuts, screws, washers, and custom fasteners from M4 to M36, catering to industries such as construction, automotive, infrastructure, oil & gas, and heavy engineering.
